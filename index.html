About the College

SNS College of Education started in 2007-2008, is one of the best Co- Educational self financing Education Colleges. It is affiliated to Tamil Nadu Teachers Education University, Chennai-5. It is recognized by the NCTE, Bangalore.
It is a self-financing, co-educational, college and performs its academic duties with a motto of Sincerity, Nobility and Service (SNS). The college is located in a rural area on Sathy (NH-209) road at Coimbatore. It offers fourteen undergraduate courses, six postgraduate courses and seven research programmes.
